The target audience involves every individuals dwelling in the shores of Sierra Leone. This include but not limited to foreigners, tourist, fishermen, lumber jacks etc.

senerio 1

With industrialization come opportunities as well as challenges. The challenges include coping with higher temperatures, extreme weather conditions, changing human life styles and changing philosophies. Due to these challenges, industrialization must take into account climate change
